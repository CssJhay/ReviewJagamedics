DESCRIPTION

Whoever is in charge of what others eat has a big role to play in health. Our Food Handlers Test is designed to ensure that those tasked with handling food do not pose any risk to others. We test over a wide range of parameters from body fluids to physical examinations.
Food Handlers’ Test is a set of tests and medical examination that certifies workers handling food, drinks, confectioneries and medicines for medical fitness.
