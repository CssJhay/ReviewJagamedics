Welcome to Jagamedics

(Jagamedics)  Jagamedics diagnostics centre is a business arm of Jagamedics Medical Services. Accredited and committed to providing laboratory testing, information and services that are crucial to physicians and other healthcare professionals, supporting them with accurate and timely diagnostics. This aids treatment decision, better healthcare and improved clinical outcome. In line with the global occupation health and safety policy, a periodic nanny medical services screening is advocated to ensure that nannies are healthy enough to safely operate in the environment (home) with the employer and other people. This is a periodic screening with the purpose of promoting health and safety in the workplaces.
